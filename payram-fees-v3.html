Before You Switch

The questions that stop switchers

What happens to the rate after the campaign ends?

The 0.3% rate applies for the campaign period, after which standard pricing resumes. We will notify you in advance, and there is no lock-in either way. The infrastructure stays yours regardless.

Do my customers need to understand crypto?

They see a normal checkout with a QR code or wallet button. Anyone with a wallet or exchange app can pay in a few taps, and you can keep cards running in parallel for everyone else.

What about volatility?

Settle in stablecoins like USDT or USDC. The amount your customer pays is the amount you hold, with no exposure to price swings.

Is 0.3% really the whole fee?

Yes. No setup fees, no monthly minimums, no chargeback fees, no reserves. The only cost outside the 0.3% is standard blockchain network gas, which is typically cents on the chains we recommend.

I run a high-risk business. Will I get rejected?

There is no application to reject. PayRam is self-hosted software, so there is no acquirer reviewing your vertical. iGaming, supplements, adult, and other high-risk merchants deploy the same way everyone else does.

Do I have to drop my current processor?

No. Most merchants run PayRam alongside their existing rails, route crypto-willing customers through it at 0.3%, and expand from there. Switching is gradual, not a cliff.
